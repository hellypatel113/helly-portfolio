४ § 04 · Roots

From Surat. A city of merchants, makers, and quiet craft.

I'm from Gujarat, a place where commerce, craft, and continuity have lived together for centuries. Surat traded textiles with the world long before "global e-commerce" was a phrase, and Patola weavers have been engineering tessellations into silk for nine hundred years.

That heritage is part of how I work: methodical, patient, focused on the thing that lasts after the launch. I bring that same steadiness to the storefronts I ship.

Outside of code, I'm rooted in this culture: festivals, food, family, the quiet rhythm of a Gujarati home. The diya on Diwali. The aarti bell. A grandmother's recipe written in a notebook.

Engineering, for me, is a kind of devotion: a careful, daily practice. The Gita says it well:

योगः कर्मसु कौशलम् "yogaḥ karmasu kauśalam" · yoga is excellence in action.
